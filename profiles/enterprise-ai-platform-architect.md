# Job Profile: Enterprise AI Platform / Internal Solutions Architect

*Created: 2026-02-16*

## Profile Overview

**Target Role Types**:
- Enterprise AI Platform Architect
- AI Solutions Architect (Internal)
- GenAI Platform Advisor
- ML Platform Lead
- AI Enablement Architect
- AI Platform Engineer (Senior)

**Industries/Sectors**:
- Large enterprises (any industry) building internal AI capabilities
- Financial services (banks, insurance, fintech)
- Tech companies (internal tooling)
- Healthcare systems
- Manufacturing / Industrial companies

**Company Size Preference**:
- Enterprise: 1,000+ employees (need internal platforms at scale)
- Mid-size with mature tech organizations: 500-1,000

## Core Value Proposition

Senior AI platform architect who builds and enables internal AI capabilities across an organization. Bridges technical platform implementation with business enablement, focusing on helping employees work better through AI-powered productivity tools, workflow automation, and intelligent systems.

Combines deep technical expertise (GenAI, ML platforms, cloud architecture, DevOps) with enterprise enablement skills. Can work across IT, business units, and data science teams to deliver platforms that balance innovation with governance, security, and scalability.

Age and seniority are assets - enterprises want stability, judgment, and someone who won't chase every shiny new AI toy. "Make things work reliably" > "disrupt everything."

## Key Experience Areas

1. **AI Platform Implementation & Operations**:
   - GenAI platform deployment (Azure OpenAI, VertexAI)
   - ML platform infrastructure and tooling
   - Platform monitoring, observability, and performance
   - DevOps/MLOps best practices
   - Multi-cloud platform management

2. **Enterprise Enablement & Onboarding**:
   - Working with business units to onboard onto platforms
   - Developer and data scientist enablement
   - Internal training and documentation
   - Use case identification and prioritization
   - Adoption metrics and success measurement

3. **Platform Architecture & Integration**:
   - Integration with 100+ internal enterprise systems
   - Enterprise data architecture (data lakes, warehouses, governance)
   - API design and microservices architecture
   - Security, compliance, and access controls
   - Scalability and cost optimization

4. **Internal Productivity Use Cases**:
   - Enterprise search and knowledge management
   - Chatbots and conversational AI
   - Document intelligence and automation
   - Code generation and developer productivity
   - Business process automation

## Technical Skills & Tools

- **GenAI Platforms**: Azure OpenAI, GCP VertexAI, model selection
- **Development**: Python, JavaScript, APIs, microservices, SQL (advanced data extraction, transformation)
- **Platform/DevOps**: Terraform, Ansible, GitHub Actions, CI/CD, containers (Docker, K8s)
- **Cloud**: AWS, Azure, GCP (multi-cloud experience)
- **Data Engineering**: Data pipelines, ETL, data integration, data governance, SQL-based data modeling
- **BI & Analytics Platforms**: Pyramid Analytics, Jaspersoft JasperReports Server, TIBCO Spotfire (competitive with Tableau/Power BI), dashboard development, reporting platforms
- **ML/AI**: LLMOps, model lifecycle management, evaluation frameworks
- **Enterprise Tools**: Integration with ERP, CRM, HR systems, collaboration tools

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Roles requiring deep knowledge of specific enterprise systems you don't have
- Positions expecting 24/7 on-call rotation (clarify expectations)
- Roles that are actually engineering manager positions in disguise
- "Innovation lab" roles with no production deployment responsibility
- Positions with unclear organizational support or budget

## Interview Prep Notes

**Key talking points:**
1. GenAI platform implementation at scale
2. Enterprise integration challenges and solutions (200+ systems at Planet)
3. Balancing innovation with governance/security
4. Platform adoption strategies and metrics
5. Cross-functional enablement approach
6. Cost management and optimization for AI platforms
7. Production reliability and operational excellence

**Questions to ask:**
- What's the current state of AI adoption across the organization?
- What are the biggest integration challenges? (enterprise systems, data access)
- How is the platform team structured? Centralized or federated?
- What does success look like in first 6 months? First year?
- What's the governance model for AI deployments?
- How is the balance between innovation/experimentation and production stability managed?
- What's the relationship between platform team and business units?
- How is platform adoption measured and incentivized?
- What's the cloud strategy? (Multi-cloud or primary cloud?)

**Technical depth questions:**
- What GenAI models/platforms are currently in use?
- What's the data architecture? (data lake, warehouse, governance approach)
- How are security and access controls implemented?
- What's the DevOps/MLOps maturity level?
- How is model evaluation and monitoring handled?
