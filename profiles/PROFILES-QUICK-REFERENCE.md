## Quick Matching Logic

**Location Filter**: Remote US, SF Bay Area hybrid/onsite, <25% travel

**Profile Selection Priority**:
1. Look for role type keywords (pre-sales, customer success, governance, analytics, architect)
2. Check technical domain (AI/ML, data/analytics, platforms)
3. Match IC vs management preference (prefer IC or player-coach)
4. Consider compensation and company stage

**Common Disqualifiers**:
- Requires Master's/PhD (have Bachelor's)
- Product-specific expertise (e.g., specific vendor platform knowledge)
- Heavy travel (>25%)
- Pure people management (10+ reports)
- Wrong technical domain (e.g., security tools vs AI/data)
- Location outside criteria
