# Job Profile: Pre-Sales / Solutions Engineer (AI, Data, Analytics)

*Created: 2026-02-16*

## Profile Overview

**Target Role Types**:
- Pre-Sales Architect
- Solutions Engineer (AI/Data/Analytics)
- Sales Engineer
- Technical Pre-Sales Architect

**Industries/Sectors**:
- B2B SaaS (AI platforms, data platforms, analytics tools)
- Enterprise software vendors
- Cloud platform providers
- AI/ML platform vendors

**Company Size Preference**:
- Vendors: 200-5,000 employees (established products, growing sales teams)
- Can also work at larger enterprises with internal "vendor" model

## Core Value Proposition

Senior technical professional who bridges complex AI/data architecture with business value and customer outcomes. Proven ability to lead technical discovery, design compelling solutions, and de-risk enterprise deals through credible architecture and stakeholder engagement.

Combines deep AI/analytics/data architecture experience with strong presentation and storytelling skills. Can engage credibly with CIOs, CTOs, and business leaders while translating technical complexity into business value. Experience supporting complex, multi-stakeholder enterprise deals.

Age and seniority are assets here - customers value experience and judgment over "high energy." This is a trust-building role where maturity matters.

## Key Experience Areas

1. **Solution Architecture & Design**:
   - End-to-end solution architecture with AI/data integration points
   - Scalable, secure AI-enabled architectures
   - Cloud, data, integrations, and AI platforms
   - Reference architectures and reusable solution patterns

2. **Sales & Business Development** (LatticeFlow, Drawing Management, Pyramid, Jaspersoft/TIBCO):
   - Driving revenue growth by identifying and closing business opportunities
   - Developing proposals, pricing strategies, and client-tailored sales pitches
   - Supporting complex enterprise sales cycles
   - Technical sales as part of solution architect responsibilities

3. **Thought Leadership & Content**:
   - Developing sales and marketing content
   - Leading webinars and speaking at conferences
   - Creating technical demonstrations and enablement materials
   - Building credibility through public speaking and content creation

4. **Technical Discovery & Pre-Sales**:
   - Business Analysis of the customer's business, organization, technology and use cases: input for solution architecture
   - Leading technical discovery during sales process
   - Qualifying opportunities and shaping deal strategies
   - Technical feasibility validation
   - RFP/RFI responses with technical depth
   - POV/POC scoping and execution

5. **Customer Engagement**:
   - Demos and technical presentations
   - Security, privacy, compliance discussions
   - Articulating AI-led transformation and value to technical and executive stakeholders
   - Identifying and mitigating deployment risks early

## Technical Skills & Tools

- **AI/ML Platforms**: OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, GCP VertexAI, LLM platforms
- **Architecture**: Solution architecture, enterprise architecture, cloud architecture
- **Cloud Platforms**: AWS, Azure (limited)
- **Data & Analytics**: Data pipelines, analytics platforms, data governance
- **Development**: Python, APIs, microservices architecture
- **DevOps**: CI/CD, MLOps, deployment automation

## Red Flags to Watch For

- "Junior" or "Associate" in title
- Heavy quota-carrying sales role (unless you want that)
- Pure inside sales or SDR roles
- Roles requiring 50%+ travel (unless acceptable to you)
- Startup pre-sales where you're employee #1-5 in sales
