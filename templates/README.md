# Content Templates

This directory contains reusable content modules for your applications. Building this library makes customization faster while maintaining consistency and your authentic voice.

## What to Store Here

### Resume Bullets
Strong achievement statements you can reuse or adapt:
- Technical accomplishments
- Leadership examples
- Project successes
- Metrics and impact statements

### Cover Letter Paragraphs
Proven paragraphs about:
- Your background and experience
- Specific projects or achievements
- Why you're interested in certain types of companies/roles
- Your approach to work

### Skills & Keywords
Lists of your actual skills organized by:
- Technical tools and platforms
- Methodologies and frameworks
- Domain knowledge
- Soft skills

## Organization Strategies

### Option 1: By Topic
```
technical-achievements.md

```

### Option 2: By Profile
```
product-manager-bullets.md

```

```
achievements/
  technical.md

  opening-statements.md

```

Choose what works for your brain.

## How to Use

1. **When writing a strong bullet/paragraph**: Save it here
2. **When customizing an application**: Browse relevant templates
3. **Copy and adapt**: Never just paste - adjust for the specific role
4. **Maintain your voice**: Read aloud to ensure it still sounds like you

## Template File Format

Use markdown with clear sections:

```markdown
# [Category]

## [Subsection]

### [Template Name]
Context: [When to use this]

[Template content]

Variations:
- [Alternative phrasing]
- [Different emphasis]

---
```

## Quality Guidelines

- **Authentic voice**: Sounds like you talking
- **Specific**: Real examples, not generic statements
- **Honest**: Claims you can back up with evidence
- **Flexible**: Easy to adapt to different contexts
- **Polished**: Well-written and error-free

## Building Your Library

Start small and grow organically:
1. Begin with your strongest 5-10 bullets from your current resume
2. Add 2-3 cover letter paragraphs that represent your best work
3. Each time you write something you like, add it here
4. Review and refine quarterly

Over time, this becomes a powerful asset for efficient, high-quality applications.
