## Template Usage Tips

1. **Adapt metrics**: Only use metrics you can defend with examples
2. **Keep your voice**: Read aloud - if it doesn't sound like you, revise
3. **Context matters**: Choose emphasis (technical/business/scale) based on role
4. **Combine bullets**: Often you can merge 2-3 bullets for stronger impact
5. **Industry swap**: Replace "financial services" with target industry as needed
