Every resume must include Education and Certifications sections at the bottom, after Earlier Career.

**Standard format:** Copy the Education and Certifications sections verbatim from `$APPLICANT_DIR/profiles/EXPERIENCE-REFERENCE.md`. Do not paraphrase, reorder, or abbreviate.

**Why:** All existing resumes include these sections. Omitting them is inconsistent and misses information employers and ATS systems explicitly require.

**How to apply:** Always include these two sections at the bottom of every resume .md, regardless of profile or role type. Do not omit them even when the role doesn't explicitly require certifications.
