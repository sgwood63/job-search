**Rule 1: Strict reverse chronological order**
All roles included in a resume must appear most-recent-first. Skipping roles is acceptable; displaying them out of order is not.

**Rule 2: Section heading is "Relevant Experience"**
The experience section must always be headed `## RELEVANT EXPERIENCE`, not `## EXPERIENCE` or `## PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE`.

**Rule 3: Earlier Career is a subsection under Relevant Experience**
All roles before 2010 must be grouped under an `### Earlier Career` (or `## EARLIER CAREER`) sub-heading. These include: Founding Architect (2005–2010), GalenWorks (2003–2005), Consulting (1999–2003), Financial Services Technology (1985–1999). Within Earlier Career, also maintain reverse chronological order.

**Rule 4: Earlier Career roles are in reverse order within the subsection**
- Founding Architect (2005–2010) before GalenWorks (2003–2005)
- GalenWorks (2003–2005) before Consulting (1999–2003)
- Consulting (1999–2003) before Financial Services Technology (1985–1999)

**Why:** Consistent structure makes documents scannable and prevents timeline confusion. Pre-2010 roles are appropriately compressed into Earlier Career without cluttering the main section.

**How to apply:**
- Use `## RELEVANT EXPERIENCE` always
- Add `### Earlier Career` or `## EARLIER CAREER` before any pre-2010 role
- Founding Architect always comes first within Earlier Career
- Never place a pre-2010 role in the main section above a post-2010 role
